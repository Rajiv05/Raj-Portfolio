Restaurant Ordering App. Yoobee Project
Overview

The aim for this project is to think of a way to minimise the use of waiters/waitresses in a restaurant (sit down to eat) environment and to engage people in a different way which will give them a different and enjoyable experience which ultimately will give people a reason to return and refer other people.

Colour Scheme & Typefaces

- Colours

I chose a very minimal colour scheme with soft grey's, white and black to follow the restaurants elegant aesthetic.

- Typefaces

Raleway is an elegant sans-serif typeface family. I chose this typeface as the shape and simplicity suited the layout and colours of the overall website. Medium weight for headers and thin weight for body.

Key Features

NFC Login

Tap the NFC sticker with your phone to login and logout if you have an android based phone supported with nfc.

Points

The idea of having a points system encourages people to come back and refer friends because people can choose to either use their points for a discount or share their points with a friend via email.

QR Code Login

Scan the table with a QR code located on the table top to login if you dont have nfc capabilities such as Apple phones.

Favourites Page

Favourites page for customers to add to their favourites.

Responsive

Ipad & mobile friendly only
