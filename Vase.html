Vase

Unitec - 1st Year - Semester 2 - 2012

Photography - Rajiv Narandas

The Brief for this assignment was called the Multiplier where we had to design something that related to “multiple”. Therefore I took that idea and used inspiration as a multiple. I chose to work with ceramics because it was one of the first tools of model making and I found it really interesting through the process. The reason why I chose triangles were because of three reasons, stregnth, movement and the future. My inspiration for this vase were the Pyramids, the Stonehenge (focal point), the Tangram, Jorn Utzon’s Sydney Opera House, Frank Lloyd Wright’s Guggenheim Museum staircase and Zaha Hadid architecture
