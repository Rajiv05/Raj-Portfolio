Haworth Application

Unitec - 2nd Year - Semester 1 - 2013

This was a collaborative assignment consisting of two people. We worked with a company called Haworth which is owned by Europlan. We got given a brief to design an accessory for their very own Infinity Range (an office desk and other small accessories). My partner and I decided to design an App. The app had two main physical objects which are essential to the design, a smartphone/tablet and an NFC sticker. The NFC sticker allows you to tap your android based smartphone to it and the app will open. This app is meant for a hot desking business situation where you can locate available desks, locate employees, a desk booking system, a meeting system and a desk availability system. It is designed to make the office environment more flexible and time saving.
